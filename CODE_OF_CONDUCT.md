# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Arborist to be a welcoming, harassment-free community for everyone,
regardless of background or identity. As contributors and maintainers, we
pledge to make participation a positive experience and to act in ways that
foster an open, friendly, and respectful environment.

## Our standard

This project has adopted the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1** as its code
of conduct. The full text describes the expected behavior, unacceptable
behavior, enforcement responsibilities, and enforcement guidelines, and is
available here:

- https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

In short: be respectful and considerate, assume good faith, give and accept
constructive feedback gracefully, and focus on what is best for the community.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces (the repository, issues,
pull requests, and discussions) and when an individual is officially
representing the project in public spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it privately
to the maintainers at the contact address listed on the repository profile.
All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly, and the
privacy and security of the reporter will be respected.
